Structural Timber Drying Service

Auckland's wet winters regularly push timber moisture content above the allowable threshold. Framing must reach ≤20% MC before wall linings can be installed and your building inspection can proceed. Waiting for it to dry naturally can cost weeks on your programme.

Structural timber drying is a service we specialise in. With over 10 years in the industry, we've developed what we believe to be a process at the cutting edge of getting frames dry in the quickest possible timeframe. Our method is grounded in the IICRC S500 Standard for Professional Water Damage Restoration and the IICRC Structural Drying principles — internationally recognised benchmarks for effective, evidence-based moisture remediation. — IICRC S500, 5th Edition; IICRC Applied Structural Drying Technician (ASD) Programme

Central to our process is the use of Zipwall drying chambers — a controlled-environment approach that concentrates airflow and dehumidification directly around affected framing. This method is not widely used in Auckland, and we believe it will be a genuine game changer for builders and contractors who can't afford to wait.
